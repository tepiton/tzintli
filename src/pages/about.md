{{title}}

[tzintli](https://nahuatl.wired-humanities.org/content/tzintli) base,
foundation, starting point; buttocks, bottom, anus (see Karttunen); 
pictured visually in glyphs as the lower half of the body, 
in place names tzintli can also refer to a lower place, 
such as at the foot of a hill, or a smaller place, 
given that -tzin can be a diminutive and not just a reverential
